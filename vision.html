Our Vision

eKosmos Services is a Pvt. Ltd. Company registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs Government of India, to provide businesses and individuals with analysis, consultancy, and digital services. We empower individuals, government agencies and enterprises both within and outside India (particularly for the South Asian and South East Asian region and Africa).

Why are we unique in who we are:

We are a cutting-edge organisation which believes that Sustainable Development is intrinsic to the life of each living being on this planet. While sustainable human development is an issue that has been in focus for a long time, few if any have explored its connections with technology and its application. We are focussed on how best Data Science, AI and other frontier technologies can best be applied for advancing Sustainable Development. We believe that innovative, data-driven approaches and integration of advanced technologies like Data Science, AI, IoT, and machine learning can provide comprehensive solutions for governments, corporations, and communities. For this we are aggressively fostering partnerships, building capacities, and continuously innovating. We strive to be a trusted partner in the global effort to advance Sustainable Development.

Our Mission

Is to advance sustainable development globally through the use of cutting-edge technology and tools like Data Science, AI and other frontier technologies.

Our Vision

A world with more equality, less vulnerability, and more human development through the use of technology; where better policy and decision making enable meaningful progress toward the SDGs.
